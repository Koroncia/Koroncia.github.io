Why Taiwan?

I’ve been getting this question frequently enough during past 7 years of living there. Unsurprisingly, it never comes from other foreigners who just like me have lived here for a while. Instead, it is usually asked by someone without any clue of this place, or – on the contrary – Taiwanese, who have not yet explored foreign land and surely couldn’t comprehend how different world is elsewhere. As I spent a quarter of my life in Taiwan, it’s the right time to finally elaborate on my phenomenal attraction to this island!
